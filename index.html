Story

Superdog needs your help!
...The only way out is...
In order to save the animals in danger, he needs to catch as many fruits and vegetables as he can. Hopefully with enough veggies he can save his friends!
Good Luck!

The goal

Your goal is to catch all the fruits and veggies that comes in your way and avoid the junk food!

You lose when

  • Superhero eats more than 3 junk items.
  • You get hit more than twice
  • You splash a tomato into a clotheslines
